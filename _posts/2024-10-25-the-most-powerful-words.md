#### The Most Powerful Words

* 'I love you' might be the most powerful words spoken followed closely by "I was wrong" and "I'm sorry".

* Living an honest and examined life is not easier, it gets more painful as you encounter your own character weaknesses and flaws. But as you build that muscle and the deeper you go the sooner you will encounter the "I was wrong" or the "I'm sorry".

* It allows you to process the mistake and to correct it. Not damming up energy in your being to block your heart chakras.

* Taking complete ownership of your life while painful will lead to tremendous breakthroughs.

* Take the pain of the mistake and the loss and move on. Nothing left to do but to keep moving.
