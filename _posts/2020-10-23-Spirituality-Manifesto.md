### Unifying Spirituality, What Does Spiritually Even Mean?

* Michael Singer, Ray Dalio, Christianity, Yoga - Is it possible that they all fit together? That there common thread on how to live a spiritual life or aware life but they are all using different language?

* Christianity speaks of God love as a fire and that fire being a refining tool. The importance of truth and living in the truth. 'to be real to one another is to confess your sins'.

* Greg Boyd once you become aware of how often you lie to yourself and to others and God you start to see the pattern. Examine the truthfulness of your thoughts.

* The purifying love of Gods will, the energy release of letting go Michael describes -> are they the same thing? They sure appear to be. It can take 2 weeks or it can take 2 months or it can take 2 years it's your choice. How far do you want to go?

Is this a super power of life?

A commitment to speak the truth and to let go of all of it.

#### Michael Singer

* Processing of energy, open minded discourse, experiencing life learning from those energies, not allowing energy to be trapped within you, letting go of past experiences. Keeping your heart chakra open.

* Spirituality and reality are not different, they are the same.

* Inside versus outside.

* The experience of life is the greatest teacher!

* Commit to letting go, if something isn't happening outside its not happening inside.

* Energy can pull you down. Do not block the energy from pulling you down.
